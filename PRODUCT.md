# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Developers, potential collaborators, and employers visiting Adarsha Acharya's personal portfolio and technical blog. They come to assess the work, read implementation-focused writing, and understand the author's technical point of view.

## Product Purpose

The site presents a personal body of work through a portfolio, technical writing, and contextual chat assistance. It should make the author's work and reasoning easy to inspect without competing with the content.

## Brand Personality

Calm, technical, editorial. The site should feel considered and direct, with the confidence of a working engineer explaining real decisions.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboard styling, loud gradients, decorative AI imagery, visual effects that compete with the writing, and template-like marketing-page scaffolding.

## Design Principles

- Let the work and writing carry the page.
- Explain technical relationships with clear, inspectable visuals.
- Prefer restraint over decoration.
- Make interactions and state changes legible.
- Keep the interface useful across reading modes and devices.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA contrast requirements. Respect reduced-motion preferences and do not rely on color alone to communicate meaning.
