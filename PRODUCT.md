# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Engineers and hiring managers landing via GitHub, X, or a blog post. They want to quickly assess technical depth, writing quality, and project range. Context: desktop or laptop, researching during work hours.

## Product Purpose

Personal portfolio and technical blog for Adarsha Acharya — fullstack engineer specializing in TypeScript, React, Node, and AI-powered products. Success = visitor understands who Adarsha is and what he builds within 10 seconds, and wants to read further.

## Brand Personality

Precise · Direct · Grounded. Like a well-written technical specification — no ceremony, no fluff, quality evident in the structure. Not trying to impress, just showing the work.

## Anti-references

- Generic dark hackery portfolios (neon, glassmorphism, floating shapes)
- leerob.io clones — editorial-typographic lane (display serif + italic + ruled separators + monochromatic restraint) is saturated
- Warm cream / sand / paper backgrounds — the #1 AI slop tell
- AI-purple-on-white
- Gradient text, gradient heroes, bento grids, pill badges
- Numbered eyebrows (01 · About / 02 · Process) as section scaffolding
- Cards for everything

## Design Principles

1. **Content is the design.** Typography and whitespace do the work. No decorative chrome.
2. **Show, don't tell.** Projects, writing, and code speak. Bio is short and honest.
3. **Earn the space.** Every pixel earns its place. If removing it doesn't hurt, remove it.
4. **Readable above all.** Blog prose is the product. Reading comfort is non-negotiable.
5. **One palette, fully committed.** Cobalt is the single brand color. Used with restraint.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast. Secondary text ≥3.5:1. Reduced motion respected. No motion-gated content (sections must be visible without JS/animation completing).
